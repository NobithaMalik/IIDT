
1.Overview of Web Development
--------------------------------

A:Web development is all about building and looking after websites and web apps. This means creating the visual parts, making things clickable, and handling how the server works behind the scenes. Nowadays, it's super important for businesses and people to have an online space where they can connect with others worldwide. Web development helps with communication, advertising, and doing business online. Doing it well is key for staying ahead and growing. Plus, it encourages new ideas by letting us create cool online stuff that makes life easier and brings people together, whether it's for work or fun.
